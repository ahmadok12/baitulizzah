About the Academy

A Peaceful Place to Learn the Quran

Bait ul Izzah Quran Academy provides structured, personalized Quran and Islamic education in a warm, nurturing environment designed specifically for women and children. Our one-to-one online Zoom classes ensure every student receives full attention, progressing at their own pace with proper Tajweed, deep understanding, and love for the Deen.

With students across Pakistan, the Middle East, Europe, and beyond, we bring authentic Islamic education to your home — no matter where you are in the world.
